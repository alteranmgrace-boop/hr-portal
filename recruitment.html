Application Guidelines

Step-by-step requirements for aspiring applicants and current employees.

General Requirements (All Positions)

  • Personal Data Sheet (PDS): Fully accomplished CS Form No. 212 (Revised 2025) with Work Experience Sheet and recent passport-sized digital picture. Must be digitally or electronically signed.
  • Performance Rating: Official copy of the performance rating from the last rating period (if applicable).
  • Proof of Eligibility: Authenticated hard copy or electronic copy of CSC Eligibility or PRC License.
  • Educational Background: Official Transcript of Records and Diploma from the educational institution.

COS Applicant Specifics

  • Photocopy of Transcript of Records and Diploma.
  • Photocopy of Certificate of Employment (COE), Trainings, and Seminars attended within the last 5 years.
  • Photocopy of CSC Eligibility, PRC License, and Board Rating.
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate and PSA Marriage Certificate (if applicable).
  • Photocopy of Barangay Clearance and Police Clearance.

Application Addressed To:

STEPHANIE A. ABELLO, MD, MMHOA, FPSP
OIC - Medical Center Chief II
